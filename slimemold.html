Slime Mold Simulation

For my final coderclass project, I wanted to try and simulate the growth of the yellow slime mold, Physarum polycephalum. To execute this, I created thousands of agents, and gave each one three senors. With these sensors, they could follow each other. This way they created paths and hubs. I improved the accuracy through various rules that I am willing to tell more about if prompted.
